The 6th International Agronursing Conference 2025

July 30 - July 31
  • « Wisuda Universitas Jember Periode VIII Tahun Akademik 2024/2025

Innovating Nursing in the Digital Age: Enhancing Education, Research, and Practice

We are delighted to extend an invitation to scholars, academicians, researchers, nurses, healthcare professionals, experts, and students around the globe to participate in the 6th International Agronursing Conference, scheduled to take place on July 30-31 in Universitas Jember.

This prestigious international event aims to bring together leading minds in the field of nursing and healthcare to share knowledge, foster collaboration, and address the challenges and opportunities shaping the future of nursing practice, education, and research.

Benefit*
✔ Engage with renowned keynote speakers and thought leaders in nursing and healthcare.
✔ Present your research, innovations, and best practices through oral presentations and poster sessions.
✔ Network with peers and professionals from diverse backgrounds and specialties.
✔ Gain insights into cutting-edge advancements in nursing science and technology.
✔ Contribute to shaping the future of global healthcare delivery.

Call for Abstracts and Participation
We warmly invite you to submit abstracts on topics including but not limited to:
– Advances in Nursing Education and Training
– Evidence-Based Practice and Clinical Innovations
– Nursing Leadership and Management
– Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing
– Global Health Challenges and Solutions
– Technology and Digital Transformation in Healthcare
– Patient-Centered Care and Quality Improvement
Abstract Submission Deadline: April 30, 2025 : Link Abstract Submission
